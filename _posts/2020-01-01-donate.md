## What your support helps with

Donations are used only to keep Vedanta Times running and improving over time. Your support generally helps with:

* **Inspiration** for the writers
* **Website hosting and domain costs**
* **Publishing and editing** new articles and lecture summaries
* **Site maintenance and improvements** (readability, layout, speed, fixes)
* Keeping the site **clean and focused**: no ads, no trackers, no distractions
* Basic tools/services that support **formatting, preservation, and archiving**

This website is built and maintained by devotees as a humble offering and a study-aid for anyone who wants to read, contemplate, and return to these ideas again and again.

### Important note about independence

Vedanta Times is **independent** and **not affiliated with, operated by, sponsored by, or officially connected to** any Vedanta organization, monastery, mission, or center.
In particular, it is **not associated with Vivekananda Vedanta Society**. Any organizations, speakers, or places referenced on this site are mentioned only for context and respectful attribution.

If you wish to donate directly to a Vedanta Society or to the Ramakrishna Order, please donate through their official websites. Donations made here support **only** the upkeep and work of this independent website.
